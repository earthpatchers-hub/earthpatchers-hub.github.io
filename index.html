Community-Powered Sustainability

A platform that helps families and young people turn sustainability into real-life action through missions, safe local collaboration, and circular habits — starting in Cluj.

Explore the MVP Plan Browse Modules

Why EarthPatchers Exists

Most people want to help, but real life is busy. Sustainability often feels like too much research, too many choices, too much guilt, and not enough clarity on what actually matters. Young people feel climate anxiety, adults feel time pressure, and communities often lack a simple way to act together.

EarthPatchers exists because the missing piece isn't "more awareness." It's an easy path from intention to action, with support and trust built in.

What Makes EarthPatchers Different

🎯

Action-First

Instead of overwhelming people with information, we start with small, realistic actions that fit into everyday life.

👪

Designed for Families & Youth

Young people care deeply about the future, but they need safe spaces, clear boundaries, and meaningful progress.

♻️

Everyday Practicality

Circular habits (swap, reuse, repair) are powerful, but only work when they're easy and safe to do locally.

🌎

Local First, Then Scale

We start with one city (Cluj), build a repeatable model, then expand with community support and partnerships.

Starting in Cluj

EarthPatchers starts in Cluj to keep it real, measurable, and community-led. Once it works well locally, it becomes a model that can be repeated elsewhere.

Trust & Safety

Youth permissions, moderation, and safe exchange principles are built into every module from day one. Kids can learn and earn while sensitive interactions are handled through adult accounts.

Partnerships

EarthPatchers is designed to work alongside schools and NGOs, not replace them. We're exploring local partnerships (e.g., Creionăm Viitorul) and future collaboration goals (e.g., Tășuleasa Social) as missions scale.

MVP — 36-Month Plan

EarthPatchers is starting with a focused MVP that proves the platform works in real life, in Cluj, before we expand. This MVP has three pillars:

🎯

Missions

Real-world eco actions

Quick missions (10–20 min) and weekend missions (1–2 hours), filtered by age, interest, and time. Progress tracked with badges, points, and a personal timeline.

Missions turn climate anxiety into doable action and give families and youth a clear starting point.

🤝

Youth Community Pilot

Small, safe, meaningful

Small pilot groups in Cluj with a realistic rhythm: monthly or seasonal challenges. Clear rules, reporting, and adult/guardian involvement where needed.

Young people care deeply about their future. This gives them a safe way to contribute without overwhelm.

♻️

Safe Circular Exchange

Swap, give away, reuse

Local swap and give-away listings for Cluj. Safety-by-design: clear exchange rules, safe pickup practices, and moderation for suspicious behavior.

Circular habits are one of the fastest ways to reduce waste, but only work if people trust the system.

36-Month Timeline

Phase 1 · Months 0–12

Prove the Core Loop in Cluj

  • Launch the missions library and start tracking progress
  • Run the first youth community pilot groups with strong safety rules
  • Launch the circular exchange pilot with moderation and safe pickup guidance
  • Add the first "learning corner" (small, curated, age-aware)
Goal

Prove people return, participate, and feel safe.

Phase 2 · Months 13–24

Make It Stronger & Easier to Run

  • Improve mission quality and "mission packs" (seasonal collections)
  • Expand youth pilot groups if Phase 1 works well
  • Improve safe exchange flows (categories, templates, trust signals)
  • Grow the learning layer carefully (still staged)
Goal

Reduce admin/moderation effort while improving experience and safety.

Phase 3 · Months 25–36

Make It Stable & Scale-Ready

  • Make the three pillars robust enough for growth
  • Ensure safety workflows work at larger scale
  • Strengthen the learning layer into clear learning paths connected to missions
Goal

EarthPatchers becomes repeatable as a model, starting from Cluj.

The "Globe" Is Staged

Our future learning experience (an "Encarta-like" Earth globe/map) is added step-by-step as a learning layer that supports Missions:

  • Phase 1: A small learning corner (simple, clear, age-aware)
  • Phase 2: A richer learning map with carefully chosen topics
  • Phase 3: More complete learning paths (still focused on clarity and reliability)

We want learning to be trustworthy and understandable, not flashy and confusing.

What the MVP Does NOT Include

EarthPatchers has many future ideas (eco shops directory, idea backlog, games, city expansion tools). These are not part of the MVP and will be developed as Next Steps, so people don't confuse the MVP with everything we might build.

What Success Looks Like

  • ✅ Missions are completed and people return for more
  • 🔐 Youth participation is safe, supportive, and meaningful
  • ♻️ Circular exchange works smoothly with trust and moderation
  • 📚 The staged learning layer helps people understand "why" and "what to do next"
  • 🏙️ Cluj becomes a strong foundation for future expansion

In MVP

Missions HQ

Missions to Save the Earth

The central hub for eco missions — micro, mini, and weekend challenges filtered by area, age, and interests. Short missions designed for families and schools, with badges, points, and visible progress.

Why It Matters

Missions turn climate anxiety into doable action. They give families and youth a clear starting point with a habit-first approach: 1–3 missions per month is a realistic, sustainable target.

Key Features

  • Quick missions (10–20 min) and weekend missions (1–2 hours)
  • Filtered by age group, interest, and time available
  • Progress tracked with badges, points, and a personal timeline
  • Seasonal mission packs and partner mission imports (NGOs, schools)
  • Habit-first approach: built to launch in small steps, then connect into one journey

Market Hut

Swap & Reuse

Swaps, giveaways, and reuse, plus a future-friendly "borrow library" (tools, books, kids gear). A practical community hook that keeps people coming back.

  • Safe-by-design: adult-managed exchanges, clear rules, safe pickup points
  • Missions connect directly to circular habits: donate, repair, reuse
  • Future: "borrow library" for tools, books, and kids gear

The Green Wall

Community Ideas & Skills

A public startup-style idea backlog where the community votes on Impact + Feasibility. People join ideas with their skills, and ideas rise as teams form and funding appears.

Why It Matters

Good ideas need more than enthusiasm — they need skills, teamwork, and sometimes funding. The Green Wall turns community energy into real projects.

Key Features

  • Public idea backlog with community voting (Impact + Feasibility)
  • Join ideas with your skills: design, education, dev, legal, ops
  • Ideas rise when teams form and funding/mentors appear
  • Optional angel/sponsor pathway for ideas that need funding

Safety Desk

Moderation & Trust

Safety, moderation, reporting, permissions, and clear community rules — especially for kids. Plain-language policies that everyone can understand.

Why It Matters

For a platform involving youth, safety isn't an add-on — it's the foundation. The Safety Desk ensures every interaction is protected, transparent, and age-appropriate.

Key Features

  • Kids can learn and earn; sensitive interactions via adult accounts
  • Anti-spam/anti-scam basics, dispute flow, simple audit trail
  • Plain-language policies: what's allowed, what isn't, how we respond
  • Clear reporting and moderation workflows

Patcher Camp

The Game

A cooperative game across climate zones where youth solve real problems using skills-as-superpowers. Elements: Earth, Water, Fire, Wind.

Why It Matters

Games make learning irresistible. Patcher Camp connects play to real-world action, helping young people see their skills as superpowers for the planet.

Key Features

  • Cooperative gameplay across climate zones
  • Four elements: Earth (materials), Water (resources), Fire (energy), Wind (mobility)
  • Wizard Tent: zero-waste recipes + calm debrief space (ages 14–18)
  • Tiny real-life tasks (cleaning, repair, cooking, upcycling) linked to missions

City Map + City Funds

City Expansion

About Us

Our Mission

To make sustainable living feel doable, social, and rewarding by giving people clear actions (missions), a safe community to learn together, and practical ways to reuse instead of waste.

Our Vision

A world where doing good for the planet is as normal as brushing your teeth: simple habits, local support, and young people growing up with the skills and confidence to protect their future.

Over time, EarthPatchers can expand city-by-city into a network of communities that take measurable local action, learn why it matters in an age-appropriate way, and make circular living accessible to everyone.

Why EarthPatchers

The missing piece isn't more awareness. It's an easy path from intention to action, with support and trust built in. EarthPatchers bridges the gap between wanting to help and knowing how.

Our Values

🌱

Sustainability

Real action over awareness campaigns. Small steps that add up to lasting change.

🤝

Community

Local support, shared missions, and the power of acting together.

🔎

Transparency

Open plans, community voting, and clear funding accountability.

🔐

Child Safety

Privacy-by-design, age-appropriate content, and guardian involvement at every step.

How We Work

We work through partnerships with schools and NGOs, pilot programs in Cluj, and community voting to decide what gets built next. Everything starts small, gets tested, and grows based on real feedback.
